Key Stats

  • Total Sales: $1.92M
  • Total Profit: $224K
  • Units Sold: 25,268
  • Top State: California
  • Top Category: Technology

Insights

  • April recorded the highest sales; January the lowest.
  • Technology was the most profitable category.
  • Alexandria and Agawam had losses despite steady sales.
  • Consumer segment led in revenue; Corporate had higher average value per order.

Business Recommendations

  • Reprice or remove low-margin items like Bookcases and Envelopes.
  • Promote tech products through seasonal campaigns.
  • Focus on corporate and small business customers.
  • Reduce delivery costs in loss-heavy regions.
